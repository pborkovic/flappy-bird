\chapter{Game Entities}

\section{Bird}

The player-controlled entity implemented as a \texttt{CharacterBody2D}.

\subsection{Physics Constants}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|l|l|l|}
\hline
\textbf{Parameter} & \textbf{Value} & \textbf{Description} \\
\hline
Gravity & 980.0 & Downward acceleration (px/s\textsuperscript{2}) \\
JumpVelocity & -350.0 & Upward impulse on input \\
MaxRotationDown & $\pi/2$ & Maximum downward tilt (90\textdegree) \\
MaxRotationUp & $-\pi/6$ & Maximum upward tilt (-30\textdegree) \\
RotationSpeed & 0.003 & Rotation interpolation factor \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\caption{Bird physics parameters}
\end{table}

\subsection{Input Handling}

The bird responds to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \texttt{ui\_accept} action
    \item Spacebar (\texttt{Key.Space})
    \item Left mouse button (\texttt{MouseButton.Left})
\end{itemize}

\subsection{State Management}

\begin{lstlisting}[language={[Sharp]C}]
public void Kill()    // Sets _isAlive = false
public void Reset()   // Restores initial state
public bool IsAlive() // State getter
\end{lstlisting}

\section{Pipe}

Obstacle entity implemented as \texttt{Area2D} with collision detection.

\subsection{Configuration}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|l|l|l|}
\hline
\textbf{Parameter} & \textbf{Value} & \textbf{Description} \\
\hline
ScrollSpeed & 150.0 (static) & Horizontal movement speed \\
DestroyPositionX & -200.0 & X position for cleanup \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\caption{Pipe configuration}
\end{table}

\subsection{Signals}

\begin{lstlisting}[language={[Sharp]C}]
public delegate void PipePassedEventHandler();
\end{lstlisting}

Emitted when pipe passes the bird's X position (100px threshold).

\subsection{Collision Handling}

On \texttt{BodyEntered}: If body is \texttt{Bird}, calls \texttt{bird.Kill()}.

\section{Ground}

Static collision boundary implemented as \texttt{StaticBody2D}.

\begin{itemize}
    \item Position: Y = 1030
    \item Collision shape: Rectangle 1920 $\times$ 100
    \item Visual: \texttt{TextureRect} with \texttt{borderBG.png}
\end{itemize}
